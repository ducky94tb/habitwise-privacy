Delete Your Account

HabitWise, by Duc Dinh

This page explains how to permanently delete your HabitWise account and the data associated with it. Deletion is immediate, free, and irreversible.

Delete from within the app (recommended)

  1. Open HabitWise.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion.

Your account and all synced data are removed from our servers immediately.

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed or can't sign in, email ducdv.bk94@gmail.com from the email address your account is registered under, with the subject "HabitWise account deletion request". We'll verify the request and delete the account within 30 days, and confirm by reply.

What gets deleted

  • Your account, including your email address and login credentials.
  • All synced habit data: habits, completion history, and challenges.

No data is retained after deletion — there is no partial deletion or deactivation option, and we keep no backups of deleted accounts.

Data stored only on your device

If you used HabitWise without signing in, your data exists only on your device and we never had access to it. Uninstalling the app removes it.

More information

See the Privacy Policy for full details on what data HabitWise collects, or the Support page for other questions.
