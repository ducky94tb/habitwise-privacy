Common questions

  • Do I need an account? No. HabitWise works fully offline. Signing in is only needed if you want your habits backed up and synced across devices.
  • How do I delete my account and data? Open the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This is immediate and permanent. See the account deletion page for details and an email-based option.
  • How do I change reminder times? On the Manage tab, tap the bell icon next to any habit.
  • How do I pause all reminders at once? Settings → Notifications → Pause all reminders.
